开展运动状态下PPG心率监测研究 Research on PPG Heart Rate Monitoring During Physical Activity

基于PPG-DaLiA公开数据集开展运动PPG信号处理研究， 利用三轴加速度信息识别运动伪影，并探索面向嵌入式 设备部署的轻量化心率估计算法。 Conducted motion PPG signal processing research using the PPG-DaLiA dataset and explored lightweight heart-rate estimation algorithms for embedded deployment.
